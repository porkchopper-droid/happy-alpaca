FARM

Welcome to our cozy alpaca barn! Here, our friendly alpacas enjoy a peaceful life, and we gather their soft fleece to create beautiful, eco-friendly products. Come say hello!

Meet our hardy mule, staying warm and cozy this winter! Wrapped in a thick winter coat, he enjoys the snowy days with ease, always ready for a friendly nuzzle and a peaceful stroll.

Our happy cow grazes peacefully by the country road, enjoying the fresh air and wide-open fields. If you pass by, you might spot her lounging in the grass, soaking up the sunshine.

Our proud rooster stands tall near the chicken coop, keeping watch over his flock. He greets the day with a hearty crow, always full of energy and ready to strut around the yard.
